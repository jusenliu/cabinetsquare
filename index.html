About Us

Since 1999, Cabinet Square has been growing exponentially in the construction industry. We are expertise in interior remodeling, both residential and commercial. We can provide both material and labor for all our jobs! We do all things related to kitchen and bath: design and decor, renovation and remodeling, appliances and products. We sell and install kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, counter tops, kitchen sinks, kitchen faucets, and closets for residential use. Contact us for FREE estimates!!!
